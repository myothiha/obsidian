# Abilities of OSINT

World Monitor is an open-source, real-time global intelligence dashboard designed for situational awareness. It aggregates diverse data streams into a unified interface, primarily focused on geopolitical monitoring, cybersecurity, and infrastructure tracking.

Based on the repository documentation, here are the high-level features of the platform:

### 1. Geospatial Intelligence (GEOINT)

- **Interactive 3D Globe:** Built with WebGL and deck.gl, featuring over 35 toggleable data layers.
- **Infrastructure Monitoring:** Real-time tracking of undersea fiber-optic cables, global pipelines, data centers, and internet outages.
- **Military & Conflict Tracking:** Monitoring of 220+ military bases, flight tracking, naval vessel locations, and active conflict hotspots.
- **Strategic Facilities:** Mapping of nuclear facilities, power plants, and climate anomalies.

### 2. AI-Powered Analysis

- **Local LLM Support:** Integration with Ollama and LM Studio allows for AI summarization and intelligence briefs to run entirely on local hardware, ensuring data privacy.
- **Automated Summarization:** AI-synthesized news briefs with "focal point detection" to filter out noise and highlight critical events.
- **Intelligence Panels:** Dedicated views for "AI Strategic Posture," "Country Instability Index," and "Infrastructure Cascade" analysis.

### 3. Real-Time Information Gathering

- **Unified News Dashboard:** Aggregates over 100 curated global news feeds (RSS) with content de-duplication.
- **Live Video Streams:** Integrated 2x2 grid for monitoring live YouTube news channels and webcams from global hotspots.
- **Prediction Markets:** Integration with platforms like Polymarket to track event probabilities and correlate them with breaking news.
- **Keyword Monitors:** User-defined keyword alerts with real-time match counts and color-coding.

### 4. Specialized Variants

- **Geopolitical Variant:** The primary dashboard for conflict, military, and infrastructure monitoring.
- **Finance Variant:** Focuses on 92 stock exchanges, central bank activities, and global FDI tracking.
- **Cyber Threat Intelligence:** A dedicated map layer for tracking cyber threats, including data from Feodo Tracker, URLhaus, and AbuseIPDB.
